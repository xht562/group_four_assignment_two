Take Action

Adopt or Foster a Pet

Give an animal the loving home they deserve. Explore our Adoptable Pets page and meet your new best friend. Not ready to adopt? Fostering saves lives by opening space for animals in need.

Volunteer Your Time

Your time is one of the most valuable gifts you can give. Join our compassionate team to help with: Dog walking and enrichment, cat care and socialization, adoption events and community outreach, and administrative and fundraising support.

Donate to Save Lives

Every donation—big or small—provides food, shelter, and medical care to homeless animals.

$25 feeds a cat for a week, $50 provides vaccines and microchipping, and $100 funds lifesaving medical treatment.

Advocate for Animals

Your voice matters. Stand with us in promoting animal welfare laws, humane treatment, and responsible pet ownership.

Sign petitions, share educational resources, and attend local advocacy events.
