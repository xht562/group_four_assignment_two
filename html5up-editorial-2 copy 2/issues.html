Issues

The Humane Society of the United States (HSUS) is dedicated to ending the most pressing forms of animal cruelty and creating a more compassionate world for all animals. We fight against puppy mills, factory farming, trophy hunting, and the cruel treatment of animals in laboratories, while also advocating for stronger animal protection laws.

  • Puppy mills
  • Factory farming
  • Trophy hunting
  • Animal testing and laboratory cruelty

Through groundbreaking investigations, public education, rescue operations, and corporate reform, HSUS works to expose cruelty and drive systemic change. Our programs provide direct care for animals in need and empower individuals, communities, and businesses to adopt humane practices that make a lasting difference.
